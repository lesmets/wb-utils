No accounts, no paywalls, no usage limits, no watermarks. Open a tool and use it.

Simple tools that respect your data

Most "free" online tools upload your files to a server, wrap the result in ads, and ask you to sign up before you can download it. WBest takes the opposite approach: every tool is a single fast page of plain HTML and JavaScript that does all of its work right on your device.

Frequently asked questions

Are these online tools really free?

Yes. Every tool is completely free, with no accounts, subscriptions, usage limits, or watermarks. Each one is a single lightweight page that costs almost nothing to run, so free stays free.

How do the tools keep my data private?

Every tool runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your text, passwords, images, and QR data are processed on your device and never uploaded, logged, or shared. You can verify it yourself: the tools keep working even with your internet connection switched off.

Do the tools work offline?

Yes. Each tool is a single self-contained page with no server calls, so once it has loaded it keeps working without a connection. You can even save a page to your device and use it anywhere.

Do I need to create an account or install anything?

No. There is nothing to sign up for, no email to enter, and no app to install. Every tool works instantly in any modern browser on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
